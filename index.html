About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Anshika Agrawal, driven by a profound passion and unwavering dedication to acquiring proficiency in diverse software. I consistently strive to elevate my skills through hands-on experience. My creative mindset enables me to seamlessly translate concepts into captivating designs. Currently pursuing a B.Tech in CSE Core from VIT Bhopal University, I actively enhance my abilities to contribute effectively to various projects and collaborative design teams.

My expertise spans across Canva, Adobe XD, CorelDraw, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Figma. Crafting design layouts and graphics is not just a task; it's a source of genuine enjoyment for me. I have a track record of practical experience, having completed a 1-month internship at TCD in the social media domain, a 4-month tenure with Info Space Export Company honing my graphic design skills, and a 1-month internship at Qualwebs as a Web Developer. Additionally, I engage in independent exploration, experimenting with creating graphics for diverse scenarios and crafting dummy posters for different organizations. This multifaceted experience, coupled with my enthusiasm for continuous learning, positions me as a valuable asset ready to contribute to dynamic design endeavors.
